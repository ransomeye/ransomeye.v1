---

## Kill Chain Stages

1. **Reconnaissance** - Information gathering
2. **Weaponization** - Malware creation
3. **Delivery** - Attack delivery
4. **Exploitation** - Vulnerability exploitation
5. **Installation** - Malware installation
6. **Command & Control** - C2 communication
7. **Actions on Objectives** - Data exfiltration/encryption

---

## Ransomware Kill Chain

1. Initial access (phishing, RDP)
2. Execution (malware execution)
3. Persistence (registry, scheduled tasks)
4. Defense evasion (anti-VM, encryption)
5. Credential access (credential dumping)
6. Discovery (network scanning)
7. Lateral movement (RDP, SMB)
8. Impact (data encryption)
